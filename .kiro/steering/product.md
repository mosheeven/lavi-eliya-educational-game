# Product Overview

Educational game for children (Lavia and Elia Even) featuring four interactive learning modes:

- **Sorting Mode**: Drag-and-drop categorization (dinosaurs, space, numbers, English letters)
- **Popping Mode**: Timed clicking game with floating balloons
- **Quiz Mode**: Multiple-choice questions with emoji-based answers
- **Math Mode (חשבון)**: Simple addition and subtraction problems for ages 5-6

The game teaches basic concepts through visual interaction, audio feedback, and Hebrew text-to-speech. Designed for young learners with parental guidance displayed at the bottom of the screen.
