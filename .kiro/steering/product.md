# Product Overview

Educational game for children (Lavia and Elia Even) featuring three interactive learning modes:

- **Sorting Mode**: Drag-and-drop categorization (dinosaurs, space, numbers, English letters)
- **English Mode (אנגלית)**: Learn English words by matching them with emoji representations
- **Math Mode (חשבון)**: Simple addition and subtraction problems for ages 5-6

The game teaches basic concepts through visual interaction, audio feedback, and Hebrew text-to-speech. Designed for young learners.
